About Me

ABOUT ME

I have a knack for going against the grain and not doing what is traditionally expected. I left my island home in Jamaica to attend college in rural Illinois. After travelling to Louisiana for graduate school and Los Angeles to start my design career, I came back to Illinois and have lived in Chicago ever since. Here's a map of my journey.

MY FAVORITE PLACES (OTHER THAN CHICAGO)

Header

LOVER'S LEAP, JAMAICA
An amazing view paired with the legend that inspired the name makes this a favorite place to visit when I'm in home. It never gets old overlooking this dramatic cliff to the ocean.

MONTERREY, MEXICO
This breathtaking Mexican city was a delight to visit. Monterrey was the highlight of a roadtrip from Baton Rouge. It showcases buildings by one my favorite architect's, Luis Barragan.

NEW ORLEANS, LA
I was in nearby Baton Rouge when Hurricane Katrina hit in 2005 and it's been great to see the city recover. My boyfriend and I visited for Mardi Gras 2012 and it was awesome!

KINGSTON, JAMAICA
Of course I had to include the place I grew up. Though it sometimes gets a bad rap, Kingston is actually a great city full of fun, excitment and many of my childhood memories.
