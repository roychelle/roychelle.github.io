About Me

  • I left Jamaica for Galesburg, IL to attend Knox College for a degree in Studio Art & Biology.
  • Combining my love for Art and Biology into a career, I received a Master's in Landscape Architecture at Louisiana State University .
  • I got my first "real" job at Mia Lehrer + Associates, which allowed me to move to Los Angeles.
  • When an opportunity at Hoerr Schaudt Landscape Architects, I moved to Chicago where I have been for 9 years.
  • After 2008s recession as many colleagues faced layoffs, I began to consider making a pivot in my career.
  • My goal was to find a fulfilling and secure career where I can be challenged to solve problems creatively.
  • 2013 I took a leap of faith and joined Startup Institute's program for Product Design.
  • I am currently a Visual User Interface Designer on a small team since 2014. This adds to my 10 years as a designer on collaborative teams.
  • Travel is a true passion mine, in 2015 my husband and I visited 14 cities including: Montego Bay (Jamaica) , Casablanca, Fez, Marrakesh (Morocco) Giza, Memphis, Cairo, Alexandria (Egypt), Austin, Philadelphia, Phoenix, Washington D.C , San Francisco and Fort Lauderdale (USA). (All overseas travel occurred during our wedding and honeymoon).
  • I love watching movies and can usually guess most of the major Oscar winners correctly each year. This makes me very useful in an office pool.
  • Equally, I love to cook and enjoy learning to prepare different cuisines and hosting dinner parties for family and friends. Jamaican food is of course my favorite.
